Start Your Feast

Malaysian Street Food

Chicken Satay

Skewers of marinated chicken grilled to perfection, served with a rich peanut sauce and cucumber relish.

Fresh and Light

Popiah (Spring Rolls)

Fresh spring rolls filled with jicama, carrots, tofu, and shrimp, wrapped in a soft crepe and served with a sweet hoisin sauce.

Zesty Salads

Kerabu Mangga (Mango Salad)

A refreshing salad made with shredded green mango, herbs, and a tangy lime dressing, topped with crushed peanuts.

Crispy Fritters

Cucur Udang (Prawn Fritters)

Golden-brown fritters made with prawns, bean sprouts, and a spiced batter, served with a sweet chili dip.

The Hearty Heart of the Meal

Malaysian Classics

Nasi Lemak

Fragrant coconut rice served with crispy anchovies, peanuts, boiled egg, cucumber, and spicy sambal.

Rich and Spicy

Beef Rendang

Tender beef slow-cooked in a rich and aromatic blend of coconut milk and spices until caramelized.

Noodle Delights

Penang Laksa

A tangy and spicy fish-based noodle soup with tamarind, herbs, and flaked mackerel, served with thick rice noodles.

Comfort Food

Hainanese Chicken Rice

Poached chicken served with fragrant rice cooked in chicken broth, accompanied by chili sauce and ginger paste.

Sweet Endings

Refreshing Treats

Cendol

A traditional Malaysian dessert made with shaved ice, green rice flour jelly, coconut milk, and palm sugar syrup.

Colorful Delights

Kuih Lapis

Steamed layered cake made with rice flour, coconut milk, and pandan, creating a soft and chewy texture.

Fried Favorites

Pisang Goreng

Crispy fried banana fritters coated in a light batter, often served with a drizzle of honey or condensed milk.

Warm Comfort

Pulut Hitam

A warm and creamy black glutinous rice pudding cooked with coconut milk and sweetened with palm sugar.
