Catering Bundles

Overview of Culinary Offerings

CaterEase Catering Services offers a wide range of culinary offerings to cater to your special occasions and events. Whether you're celebrating a birthday, a wedding, or a corporate function, our catering bundles are designed to provide a personalized and memorable experience for your guests.

Package 1: Malaysian Feast Delight

Package Details

  • Nasi Lemak - Fragrant coconut rice with sambal, crispy anchovies, peanuts, and boiled egg.
  • Chicken Satay - Grilled chicken skewers with peanut sauce.
  • Beef Rendang - Slow-cooked beef in rich coconut and spices.
  • Cendol - Refreshing dessert with shaved ice, coconut milk, and palm sugar.
  • Roti Canai - Flaky flatbread served with curry dipping sauce.
  • Ayam Percik - Grilled chicken marinated in spiced coconut sauce.

Services

  • Buffet-style serving for up to 50 guests.
  • Traditional Malaysian-themed decorations.
  • Complimentary welcome drinks (e.g., Teh Tarik).
  • Free setup of traditional music and cultural performances.
